The Prototype

About BoardEase

BoardEase is a mobile application prototype designed to help students and professionals find safe, verified, and affordable boarding houses near their location. This website serves as the comprehensive usability evaluation report of the BoardEase prototype, documenting the methods, findings, and recommendations gathered through Heuristic Evaluation and Usability Testing conducted as part of the HCI2 course at Partido State University, Goa, Camarines Sur.
